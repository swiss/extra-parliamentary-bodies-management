# Extra Parliamentary Bodies

## Introduction

This is an application for managing extra-parliamentary bodies and their members.
Evaluations in the form of statistics and publication of data on the internet as OGD.
Handling and verification of general elections and supplementary elections.
Preparation of Federal Council affairs for the election of members and reporting to parliament.
